<p class="lead">This campaign is about giving MCCTC students a fair chance to learn with the tools they already use every day.</p>

<section class="card-grid">
  <article class="card">
    <h3>Our mission</h3>
    <p>We want MCCTC to bring back a thoughtful BYOD policy that helps students learn, collaborate, and prepare for the future.</p>
  </article>

  <article class="card">
    <h3>Why it matters</h3>
    <p>Students are already using technology for research, organization, and creation. Schools should support those habits with clear expectations rather than ignore them.</p>
  </article>

  <article class="card">
    <h3>What we believe</h3>
    <p>Technology should be a bridge to better learning, not a barrier. A good BYOD policy can support both student independence and teacher guidance.</p>
  </article>
</section>

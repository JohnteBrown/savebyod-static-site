Students are already carrying the habits of modern learning into every class. They research, organize, collaborate, and create using devices they know well. A thoughtful BYOD policy would let them use those skills in a school setting without losing instructional time.

The goal is not to turn every classroom into a distraction zone. The goal is to make sure school technology supports real learning, not just a narrow set of approved tools.

That is why many students and families are asking MCCTC to bring back a clear and responsible BYOD policy.
